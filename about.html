About Durga Fire & Safety Consultants

Who We Are

A Trusted Name in Fire & Industrial Safety

Durga Fire & Safety Consultants is a trusted name in fire protection, industrial safety, emergency preparedness, and fire manpower services.

Founded on a legacy of commitment to public safety and industrial protection, we have spent over 35 years helping organizations strengthen their emergency response capabilities and maintain safe working environments.

Today, our team of 700+ trained professionals supports industries, government organizations, commercial establishments, hospitals, and institutions across Gujarat.
